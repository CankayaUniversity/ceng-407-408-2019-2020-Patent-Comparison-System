Frequently asked questions for patent comparison.

The system will examine the documents you sent us, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
What is the patent system?
The system that helps the development of the industry in order to encourage the implementation of innovations and inventions and to apply them to the industry, and to encourage those who work on this subject by giving privileges to the industry for a certain period of time, is called a patent system.
How long is the patent registration lifetime?
Patents without examination have 7 years of patents from the date of application and create rights for 20 years from the date of application. At the end of these periods, the invention becomes public domain.
How to tell if a product is like a patented product?
Even if a product is manufactured with a different design in terms of its patent-protected functions or another product image, the imitation status will be fixed. If the patent has protected a production method, the sameness of the production method will be sought rather than the product produced.
